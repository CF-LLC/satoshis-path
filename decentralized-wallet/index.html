Why Choose Self-Custody?

🔑

True Ownership

Control your private keys and truly own your Bitcoin. No third party can freeze or confiscate your funds.

🛡️

Enhanced Security

Eliminate counterparty risk. Your Bitcoin is only as secure as your personal security practices.

🌐

Censorship Resistance

Access your Bitcoin anywhere, anytime. No accounts to be closed or transactions to be blocked.

📝

Seed Phrase Security

  • •Write seed phrase on paper, never store digitally
  • •Make multiple copies and store in different locations
  • •Consider metal backup for fire/water protection
  • •Never share your seed phrase with anyone
⚙️

Wallet Setup

  • •Always buy hardware wallets from official sources
  • •Verify device authenticity before use
  • •Initialize wallet yourself, never use pre-configured devices
  • •Test recovery process with small amounts first
🛡️

Operational Security

  • •Use strong, unique passwords and PINs
  • •Keep firmware updated on hardware wallets
  • •Be cautious of phishing attempts and fake apps
  • •Consider using a dedicated computer for Bitcoin activities

Ready to Take Control of Your Bitcoin?

Start with a small amount and practice using self-custody wallets. As you become comfortable, gradually move more of your Bitcoin to your own control.
